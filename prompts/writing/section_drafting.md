# Section Drafting Prompt

Draft or revise a manuscript section for the `MNSIM-2.0` research workflow.

The section should:

- stay academically restrained
- reflect the repository's real experiment status
- avoid overstating robustness or generalization
- connect methodology to actual scripts and artifact types when helpful

Writing rules:

- prefer clear subsection structure
- use `TODO:` for missing citations or evidence
- keep hypotheses and validated observations distinct
- avoid marketing language

Suggested uses:

- problem formulation
- experiment setup
- measured-preset methodology
- result discussion
- limitation and future work sections
